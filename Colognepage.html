Welcome to Cologne

Welcome to Cologne, The city that…

Here the only beer you will find is called Kolsch, it is only brewed in cologne and is traditionally served in 0.2 litre glasses which are cylindrical. The bar man will keep refilling your glass until you put your coaster on top of it!

Crack open your beer of choice, and enjoy this city.

City History

The Dom, Cologne Cathedral

Wherever you stand in Cologne, the cathedral can be seen towering above rooftops and chimneys, 157m at its highest point.
This gothic cathedral features many colourful stain glass windows.
The cathedral was one of the only parts of the city which survived bombing in the second world war, it was extensively damaged, and became the centre of the cities rebuild.

Timeride

As we come towards the end of the historical journey, we visit Timeride.
Timeride offers an immersive experience of Cologne at the beginning of the 20th Century.
It is a virtual reality experience, taking you on a tour of the old town before the world wars, from the first electrical tram in the city.

Museums

Next on the tour, head to the Imhoff Chocolate Museum.
Learn about the process of chocolate making, from bean to bar, and taste some for yourself. If you are feeling hungry, the cafe serves delicious pancakes coated in fruit and chocolate.
For the sports fanatics, the Olympic museum is right next door. There are lots of Olympic style games and tests you can attempt, bringing out the competitive streak in everyone, it even has a rooftop football pitch.

The Views

After you have seen the sights of Cologne up close, the Triangle is the perfect way to round off the day.
If you aren’t afraid of heights that is. Take the lift up to the 29th floor, and take in the panoramic views of the city, the cathedral, the river Rhine
